About Pynest

Where Luxury Meets

Pynest curates an exceptional collection of luxury villas and premium homestays, each with private pools, nestled in the serene coastal landscapes of Pondicherry. We believe that a stay should be more than accommodation — it should be an experience that lingers.

From bougainvillea-draped French Quarter retreats to sun-drenched beachside villas, every Pynest property is hand-selected for its architecture, privacy, and effortless elegance.
